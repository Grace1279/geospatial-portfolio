## Featured Projects

### 2. LA Coastal Flood Exposure and Land Subsidence StoryMap
This ArcGIS StoryMap investigates how vertical land motion and land subsidence may increase future coastal flood exposure along the Los Angeles coastline.

**Skills demonstrated**
- ArcGIS StoryMaps
- Coastal flood exposure mapping
- Sea-level-rise scenario analysis
- Vertical land motion interpretation
- Difference mapping
- Infrastructure exposure analysis

**Key results**
- Additional flood exposure from subsidence: 7.68 km² under the 2 ft scenario
- Additional flood exposure from subsidence: 11.55 km² under the 4 ft scenario
- Additional road exposure from subsidence: 44.20 km under the 2 ft scenario
- Additional road exposure from subsidence: 60.81 km under the 4 ft scenario
